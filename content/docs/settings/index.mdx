The Settings section of MediFlux is where administrators configure how the platform behaves for your organization. Manage users, roles, locations, tax rules, and module-specific preferences from one place.

## Overview

Settings apply across modules, so changes here affect sales, inventory, purchases, and reporting workflows consistently.

## Key Capabilities

- **Organization Setup** — Configure business profile and locations
- **Users & Roles** — Control access with role-based permissions
- **Tax & Pricing** — Set default tax rates and pricing rules
- **Module Preferences** — Tune behavior for POS, inventory, and compliance features
