The Reports section of MediFlux provides analytics and exportable summaries to help you understand pharmacy performance. Review trends in revenue, stock movement, supplier activity, and operational metrics.

## Overview

Reports pull data from sales, inventory, purchases, and accounting modules so you can make decisions based on up-to-date information.

## Key Capabilities

- **Sales Reports** — Daily revenue, transaction counts, and product performance
- **Inventory Reports** — Stock valuation, expiry risk, and movement summaries
- **Purchase Reports** — Supplier spend and order fulfillment tracking
- **Export Options** — Download or share reports for accounting and management review
