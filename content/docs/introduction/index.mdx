MediFlux is a modern, **full-featured pharmacy and healthcare management platform** designed to streamline every aspect of your pharmacy operations. From point-of-sale transactions and inventory management to financial reporting and regulatory compliance, MediFlux provides an integrated solution that grows with your business.

Whether you're running a single independent pharmacy or managing a chain of locations, MediFlux adapts to your workflow. The platform combines an intuitive user interface with powerful backend capabilities, giving your team the tools they need to serve patients efficiently while maintaining full visibility into your business performance.

## Key Features

- **Pharmacy Sales & POS** — Process sales, manage prescriptions, and handle customer transactions with a streamlined point-of-sale interface built for pharmacy workflows.
- **Purchase Order Management** — Create, track, and receive purchase orders from suppliers with automated reorder points and inventory optimization.
- **Inventory Control** — Real-time inventory tracking with batch and expiry management, stock alerts, and multi-location support.
- **Accounting & Finance** — Double-entry bookkeeping, general ledger management, and comprehensive financial reporting out of the box.
- **API & Integrations** — RESTful API for integrating MediFlux with your existing systems, third-party services, and custom applications.
- **Regulatory Compliance** — Built-in support for controlled substance tracking, audit trails, and reporting requirements.

## Architecture Overview

MediFlux is built on a modern technology stack designed for reliability and performance. The platform uses a microservices architecture with a PostgreSQL database backend, ensuring data integrity and scalability. All communication between services is encrypted, and the system supports role-based access control to protect sensitive patient and financial data.

<Callout type="info">
  MediFlux supports both cloud-hosted and on-premise deployments. Choose the option that best fits your organization's security and compliance requirements.
</Callout>
