The Customers section of MediFlux helps you maintain patient and customer records linked to sales, prescriptions, and loyalty programs. Keep contact information, preferences, and transaction history in one place.

## Overview

Customer profiles connect to your POS workflow so staff can quickly look up accounts, apply discounts, and review past purchases during checkout.

## Key Capabilities

- **Customer Profiles** — Store names, contact details, and identifiers
- **Purchase History** — Review past transactions and returns
- **Loyalty & Discounts** — Attach programs and membership benefits
- **Search & Lookup** — Find customers quickly at the point of sale
