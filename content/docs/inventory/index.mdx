The Inventory section of MediFlux gives you real-time visibility into what is on hand across your pharmacy locations. Monitor stock levels, batch numbers, expiry dates, and reorder thresholds from a single dashboard.

## Overview

Inventory updates automatically when sales, purchases, returns, and adjustments are recorded. This keeps your on-hand quantities accurate without manual spreadsheet work.

## Key Capabilities

- **Stock Tracking** — View current quantities by product and location
- **Batch & Expiry** — Manage lot numbers and expiry-driven picking rules
- **Low Stock Alerts** — Get notified when items fall below reorder points
- **Adjustments** — Record damage, shrinkage, and manual corrections with audit trails
