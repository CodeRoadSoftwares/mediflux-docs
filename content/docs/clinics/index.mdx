The Clinic Module extends MediFlux to support clinic operations alongside pharmacy management. Coordinate appointments, patient visits, and dispensing workflows when your organization runs both clinical and retail pharmacy services.

## Overview

This module connects clinic activity with inventory and sales so prescriptions and clinical services stay aligned with your pharmacy operations.

## Key Capabilities

- **Clinic Profiles** — Configure clinic locations and service offerings
- **Appointments** — Schedule and manage patient visits
- **Prescription Flow** — Link clinical prescriptions to pharmacy dispensing
- **Integrated Reporting** — Review clinic and pharmacy activity together
